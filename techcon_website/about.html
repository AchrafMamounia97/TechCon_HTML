Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of developers passionate about emerging technologies. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most influential technology conferences in the world. Each year, TechCon evolves to showcase the latest innovations and foster collaboration across disciplines.

Our Mission

At TechCon, we strive to inspire innovation, support diversity in tech, and connect passionate individuals who are shaping the future. Our mission is to provide a platform where ideas thrive and people from all backgrounds can learn, share, and grow together.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Rivera

A pioneer in artificial intelligence, Dr. Rivera's work has transformed machine learning in healthcare. She spoke at TechCon 2022 about ethical AI and the future of intelligent systems.

Marcus Lin

Founder of DevSpark, Marcus is a leading voice in open-source development. His keynote in 2021 focused on empowering communities through open collaboration.

Sofia Zhang

Sofia, a cybersecurity expert and former White Hat hacker, emphasized the importance of digital safety in a rapidly evolving tech landscape during her 2020 panel appearance.
